About Us

Abbeyfield Golden Bay Incorporated is a non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing the lives of older individuals by providing safe, supportive, and affordable housing. We believe in fostering a sense of community and independence for our residents.

Our house is more than just a building; it's a home where residents can enjoy companionship, security, and a vibrant social life. We are part of the wider Abbeyfield New Zealand and international Abbeyfield family.

Our Mission

Our mission is to alleviate loneliness and insecurity for older people by providing them with their own private space within a supportive, family-like environment. We aim to offer a balance of privacy and companionship, with home-cooked meals and a dedicated house manager ensuring a comfortable and caring atmosphere.

  • Affordable accommodation
  • Companionship and community
  • Safe and secure living
  • Support for independent living
